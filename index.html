About Us

We've Been Making Healthy Food Last For 10 Years


TOSH-E-DAAN is a restaurant that offers a variety of delicious dishes made with the freshest ingredients. Our menu features a range of dishes that cater to all tastes, including double gosht mutton biryani, mutton marag with naan, dum ka murchi, mutton haleem, and badami kheer. We take pride in our food and make sure that each dish is prepared with love and care. Visit us today and treat yourself to a memorable dining experience.

Food Menu

Double Gosht Mutton Biryani

A delightful and aromatic blend of tender mutton, basmati rice, and an array of aromatic spices, slow-cooked to perfection. This biryani is sure to tantalize your taste buds and leave you asking for more.

Price: ₹ 450

Mutton Marag with Naan

A traditional and flavorful Mutton Marag, made with tender and juicy mutton pieces simmered in a rich gravy of aromatic spices, served with soft and fluffy naan.

Price: ₹ 350

Dum Ka Murchi

A slow-cooked and succulent Mutton Murchi, with the mutton pieces simmered in a rich tomato-based gravy, infused with a blend of aromatic spices, and slow-cooked in a sealed pot to retain all the flavors.

Price: ₹ 350

Mutton Haleem

A traditional and hearty stew, made with mutton, lentils, and an array of spices, slow-cooked to a creamy consistency and topped with caramelized onions, and fresh herbs.

Price: ₹ 290

Badami Kheer

A sweet and creamy dessert, made with creamy almond milk, sweetened with sugar, and infused with cardamom and saffron, this kheer is a perfect way to end a satisfying meal.

Price: ₹ 250

Apricot delight

Apricot Delight is a delectable dessert made with juicy apricots and a creamy base. This dessert is a perfect balance of sweet and tart flavors, and its smooth texture makes it a delightful treat for any dessert lover.

Price: ₹ 250
